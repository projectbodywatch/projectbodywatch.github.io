Configure your application

Show the application what your good pose is and what your bad pose is.

Notifications

Get notifications based on your pose! We will notify you for the following:
Watch your pose: We will let you know if you aren't sitting properly.

Keep it up: To motivate YOU to sit properly, we give you a sticker!

Time for a break: Break times are needed. Just click this notification to start your break.

Change settings

Change settings based on your own preference. You can change the following settings:
Mute or unmute sound.
Change time between the notifications given. (except the bad pose and break timer)
Change period between good pose checks.
